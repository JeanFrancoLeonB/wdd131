Trujillo, Peru

Trujillo is a Peruvian city, the capital of both the homonymous province and La Libertad department. Industrially, Trujillo is renowned as a hub for leather and footwear production, as well as a significant center in agribusiness. Traditionally, the city has been noted for its diverse commercial activities at both regional and national levels, serving as a pivotal gateway to Peru's northern coast and connecting provinces such as Ancash and Cajamarca, roles recognized since colonial times.
